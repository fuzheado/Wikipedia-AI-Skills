For authors, journalists, poets, and playwrights.

```wikitext
| caption          =

| notable_works    = 
| awards           = 
| spouse           =

}}
```

**Use when:** Subject is primarily an author, journalist, poet, or playwright.
**Key fields:** `genre`, `notable_works`, `period`, `awards`
